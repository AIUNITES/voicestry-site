🌠 Real-time Vocal Analysis

Hear Them. Match Them.

Play any YouTube vocalist, capture their pitch profile, then sing live and see exactly how closely you match — note by note, in real time.

How It Works
1

Load a YouTube Video

Paste any YouTube URL and the video loads in the player below.

2

Capture Reference

Play the YouTube video through your speakers. Press Capture Vocalist — your mic listens to the room and builds the vocalist's pitch profile.

3

Sing Live

Press Sing Live. Sing (or mute YouTube and sing). Your live pitch is analyzed in real time.

4

See Your Match Score

The match panel shows how close you are — pitch difference, match %, grade, and a VRN readout.

💡 Best setup: Use a phone with YouTube playing through the speaker. Hold the phone 6-12 inches from your mouth for Capture mode. Switch to Sing mode and sing normally.
💖 Earphones tip: If you use earphones, the reference capture step works less well. Better to use speakers for that step.
📾 Microphone access denied. This tool needs your mic to analyze vocals. Please allow microphone access in your browser and reload the page.
