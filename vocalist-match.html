🌠 Real-time Vocal Match

Hear Them. Match Them.

Play any YouTube vocalist, capture their pitch profile, then sing live and see exactly how closely you match — note by note, in real time.

How It Works
1

Load a YouTube Video

Paste any YouTube URL — the video loads in the player below.

2

Capture Reference

Play YouTube through your speakers. Press Capture Vocalist — your mic listens to the room and builds the vocalist's pitch profile over 12 seconds.

3

Sing Live

Press Sing Live. Sing into the mic — your pitch is analyzed and compared to the reference in real time.

4

See Your Score

Watch your match %, grade, pitch offset needle, and a VRN readout update live.

💡 Best setup: Phone with YouTube playing through the speaker. Hold the phone at arm's length during Capture, then bring it close to your mouth while singing. Earphones reduce reference quality — use speakers for that step.
📾 Microphone access denied. This tool needs mic access to analyze your voice. Please allow microphone access in your browser settings and reload the page.
